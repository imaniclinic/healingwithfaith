Youth Xplosion Partnership

YXP Mission Statement:
Youth Xplosion collaborates with strategic partners to support community members through: Food and clothing distributions, Employment readiness programs, Literacy programs, Adult engagement forums, Health education and referral services, Arts and crafts, Financial literacy, Life skills
Sunrise Breakfast
Held every 2nd Saturday of the month, Sunrise Breakfast is a community engagement program. Alongside healthy food demonstrations, children's activity stations, and hot brunch served, Sunrise Breakfast is where community members are informed about Youth Xplosion's progress, and of local resources available to them. Imani Clinic volunteers provide health-related stations, assist with food preparation, aid with children's stations, and more!
